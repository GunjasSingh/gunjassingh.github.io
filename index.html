  I'm Gunjas Singh

An undergrad at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani) in my pre final year pursuing B.E. (Hons.) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Msc. (Hons) in Economics

Research fields include- Brain Computer Interfaces and Computer Vision

Brain Computer Interface: Motor Imagery

Real time classifiaction of Motor Imagery tasks. Experiments conducted on live data on the Emotiv Epoc+ consumer headset. Working on this Lab project in association with IOT lab BITS Pilani, Dr. Vinay Chamola (Prof, BITS Pilani)

Brain Computer Interface: Emotion Analysis

Emotion Analysis on the DREAMER dataset, classifying or high and low valence, arousal and dominance on data collected subject to audio visual stimuli. LSTM architecture used to reach an accuracy of 62%.

July 2020 - September 2020

Gait Recognition

Cross-view Gait recognition from a closed circuit camera view as a biometric technique using Convolutional Neural Networks with Gait Enropy Images (GEI) as input subject to covariate factors like viewing angle and clothing. Accuracy of 74.2% achieved for +-20% change in viewing angle. Working with Dr. Pawan Ajmera (Prof, BITS Pilani)
